The other ways to organize elements on a webpage
without using using relative or absolute positioning:
Float, Clear, Clearfix, Margin, Height, Width.
*Float: places the element on the left or right side of its container.
*Clear: sets an element must be moved below cleared elements that precede it.
*Clearfix: solves a persist bug that occurs when two floated elements are stacked next to each other.
*Margin: sets the margin area on all four sides of an element.
*Height: specifies the height of an element.
*Width: sets an element's width of the content area.

BERBER STYLE CARPET

The term Berber generally refers to carpet that's overall light in color with flecks of darker colors (usually brown or gray) and woven with a distinctive loop pile that attaches to the backing and remains uncut. It's inspired by the weaving style of the Berber people of North Africa.

Are Berbers and Moors the same?

The Moors were the Muslim inhabitants of the Maghreb, the Iberian Peninsula, Sicily, and Malta during the Middle Ages. The Moors initially were the indigenous Maghrebine Berbers. The name was later also applied to Arabs.
